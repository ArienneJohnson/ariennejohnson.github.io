Brand Competitors

Purdys Chocolatiers

Strengths

For Purdy's Chocolatiers, their logo is very similar to the famous Cadbury's Chocolate Bar logo. They use purple, which is a complimentary colour to brown and commonly used for chocolate wrappers. The typeface used was decorative font, similar to script typefaces, and visually reminds the viewer of the smooth texture of chocolate.

Weaknesses

Their website design is a little complicated, and sometimes the business name can get lost in the design.

Logo Inspiration

How the logo shows what the company is about

I want to show that the chocolate company brand is a chocolate company first. I do not want the consumer to get confused on what the compay is trying to sell.

Why the logo is effective for the brand

The logo is effective because it shows that it is representing a chocolatier company. I used the main colours, red, gold and light blue, and I changes the blue background to a neutral chocolate brown.

How the branding/logo makes us stand out

The colours of the bird, which are the colours of the company, really stand out with the brown. When a consumer sees the bird wings they will immediately know that this is their company.
