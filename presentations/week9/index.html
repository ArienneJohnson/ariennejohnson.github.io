Hard - FeedBack for Chicken Mingle

1. The colour scheme is very nice and reflects romance, however the text colour can be distracting. Pick a maximum of 3 colours to use for text, and make sure it is contrasting to the background, also have the typeface reflect the loving atmosphere is the site, probably with a serif or script font.

2. The minimalism of the logo is great. Make it stand out by making it larger and more prominant.

3. The sign up button is eye catching. Make it more eye catching by having the background colour bold yet still in the colour scheme. Also center the words SIGN UP in the button.

4. By making the background colours lighter, it will make the webpage more open and welcoming, rather than having the black background.

5. Making the title a similar colour to the background makes it a little hard to see. Maybe try to change the title to white or black so it stands out more.

6. Lining up all of the text and photos makes the website more eye appealing and appear more organized. Try floating some text left or right so they are all aligned.
